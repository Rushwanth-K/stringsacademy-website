Our Story

Born from a Love
for Music

StringsAcademy was founded in 2014 by Arjun Sharma, a professional guitarist with over 15 years of stage experience. What started as weekend classes in a small room in T. Nagar has grown into Chennai's most trusted guitar learning institute.

We believe music is for everyone — not just the naturally talented. Our teaching method combines structured theory with practical playing, so students see real progress fast.
